Canada is home to breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cities and diverse cultures that come from across the globe. In 2018, my family and I decided to go on a road trip across the country to explore the different provinces and visit famous locations such as Banff. We set off in the beginning of August and completed the trip by the end of the month. This blog is to share my experiences and hopefully motivate you to visit these locations as well! I hope you enjoy!

Winnipeg - Museum for Human Rights

The Canadian Museum for Human Rights in Winnipeg is a moving experience, highlighting the importance of rights and freedoms across the world.

Learn more about the museum by visiting their official website.

Edmonton Mall

West Edmonton Mall is not just a shopping destination but a world of entertainment with an indoor water park, amusement rides, and so much more.

Jasper

Jasper National Park is a peaceful escape into nature, offering scenic views, stunning trails, and the beauty of the night sky.

Banff

Banff National Park is a jewel of the Rockies, featuring majestic mountains and crystal-clear lakes. Hiking, skiing, and wildlife spotting are just a few of the activities to enjoy here.

Butchart Garden

Butchart Garden in Victoria is a feast for the senses, featuring beautifully manicured gardens that are perfect for a peaceful day out.
