The Canadian Museum for Human Rights in Winnipeg is a moving experience, highlighting the importance of rights and freedoms across the world. Jasper is a beautiful and peacful national park. It has incredibel views, trails and I have heard it is worth visiting at night to see the starry sky! We, however, went during the day and at this time there was a lot more smoke in the air due to the forest fires happening in British Columbia. Capilano Suspension Bridge was such an exciting experience! Walking across the bridge surrounded by towering trees was both thrilling and peaceful!
